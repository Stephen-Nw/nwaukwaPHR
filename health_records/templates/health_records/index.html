What is the personal health record

The Nwaukwa Personal Health Record (PHR) is a tool to help you manage your health information digitally. With this software, you can store all your pertinent medical information online. You can also track your medications as well as medical appointments. This software allows you to create an account not just for you, but for your family as well so that they can be managed centrally.

When should I use the personal health record

After the intial setup, the PHR should be updated whenever there are changes to your health status, medications, or medical appointments. This helps to ensure that your medical information remains current and up to date.

Who can use this personal health record

At this moment, only members of the Nwaukwa family can use this personal health record. This include both immediate as well as extended members of the family. A version of this tool will be made available for public use at a later date.
